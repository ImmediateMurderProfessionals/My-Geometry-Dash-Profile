GIGGSRH99

GIGGSRH99 is an entry to the Silly Creator Contest hosted by YraX and Chloe that I made from November to December 2025 before releasing on December 15, 2025.

In the level, you start out in Geometry Dash, before getting whisked away to a faraway land and fusing popular creators GiggsRH and AlvaroPaws. The creation then turns against you, and then sends you to an alternate dimension. Can you escape?

Rating status

The level is unrated, and I am aiming for about a medium demon rate. So far it has been sent to 5 moderators, of which, to my knowledge, 4 have sent the level and 0 have rejected it.
